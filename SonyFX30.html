Is The Sony FX30 A Best Camera for Inde Filmmakers Lets Find Out

~by Punit Sabnani | World on Hands

The Sony Fx30 has some pretty exciting video features including 4k 120P and FHD 240P internal 10 Bit 4:2:2 recording. It also boasts a brand-new 26 Megapixel Super 35mm picture sensor and excellent still image quality.

The FX30 camera is capable of recording very high quality video footages and also offers innovative and unique picture profiles like S-Cinetone, HLG, Cine 4, and Slog 3 with Cine EI mode. I have utilized the FX30 for my recent video projects, and the video results are fantastic.

Cinema Line FX30 low Light Results are also out standing this camera offers Dual Base ISO Sensitivity, Camera Offers Low bas 800 and High base 2500, Depending on shooting situation you can use the both of the ISO sensitivity to get the best dynamic range + an equal noise level across the videos.
