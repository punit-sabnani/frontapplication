DJI RS3 MINI Review

~by Punit Sabnani | World on Hands

If you use a Sony camera with block lenses, the RS3 Mini is a fantastic affordable gimbal. The gimbal is quite well built, and the entire item has a luxury appearance. It also has a large touch display for controlling the gimbal modes.

This gimbal can support full-frame bodies and lightweight lenses and can carry up to 1.8KG of payload.The gimbal is quite well built, and the entire item has a luxury appearance. It also has a large touch display for controlling the gimbal modes.

Gimbal is fairly light weight - only weighs 700gr - and when you shoot videos with it you will be more creative owing to less weight. We recently made a short one-shot film where we utilised this gimbal and we received such a fantastic reception.There is also good battery life.
