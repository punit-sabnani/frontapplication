Why Everyone Loved FX6 For Filmmaking – Here are top 4 featurs.

~by Punit Sabnani | World on Hands

  • Affordability :-

    • This camera Is a most affordable camera for filmmaking as per the featurs its packed into it. In India the camera body cost approx. 6 lakh MRP Body only – In Box customer will get the FX6 Brain + 1 Battery + Top Handle + Side Gripe + High Resolution Display + DC Cable and battery Charge.

  • Sensor Size :-

    • The FX6 features a 12-megapixel Large Format\ Full Frame Sensor, which offers excellent low-light performance and 15+ dynamic range.
    This camera has dual base ISO 800/12800 – For daylight or Control Light Situation users can use 800 ISO and For Low Light Shooting use can use 12800 ISO.

  • Amazing AF :-

    • FX6 Equipped with New hybrid AF Technology which offer relabel Eye AF which not only can focus on the human eye but also can track & focus object or subject, The Focus is so preside that indemnedt filmmakers can shoot video without any focuse puller.

  • Recording Format :-

    • FX6 offers veriose rage of recording formats which allows filmmakers to manage their recording quality during the video shoot,
    Camera can record DCI 4K 60P & QFHD 120P, FHD240 with Fast AF and no pixel binning.
